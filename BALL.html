"BALL". 2021. Blender 3D Animation.

<-- -->

For "BALL", I had no specific concept in mind. I wanted to build off my former experience in Blender with "Lay With Me Beneath the Gemini Sky" and further familiarize myself with the software. With this piece, I was able to teach myself more about Blender's keyframe feature to create this simple 3D animation.

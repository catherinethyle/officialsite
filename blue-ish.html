"Blue-ish". 2020. Acrylic on fabric. 11 x 8 in.

<-- -->

"Blue-ish" is based off a two pictures that I saw one Twitter. Both were of beautiful women, had a nice composition, and pretty colors that were on both the cooler and warmer side of the spectrum. I decided to experiment with painting on fabric again and came up with "Blue-ish"- named by how the cooler colors in the piece are kind of but not quite blue. There is a woman in a tourquoise dress, boots, and baret who is outlined by gold paint, standing at the center of an vacant room. In the upper right corner, the face of a woman with a blue makeup protrudes, and she is looking towards the woman at the center of the piece. I really enjoy the composition of the whole piece and the harmoney between all the colors. There is a nice balance of space and between the warm and cool tones that I really enjoy.

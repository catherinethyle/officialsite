"Citrus & Cinnamon". 2020. Acrylic on paper. 8.25 x 8 in.

<-- -->

"Citrus & Cinnamon" is an surrealistoc acrylic painting of various citrus fruits- such as lemons, an orange, and a pomello- and hands holding onto AirPods with the Sanrio character Cinnamoroll. I came up with this piece based off of a prompt that I created for Inktober which was "word/phrase that begins with 'ci-'". I immediately thought of citrus and cinnamon- cinnamon because I originally thought the Sanrio character's name was "Cinnamonroll". I wanted to use a bright and playful color scheme for this piece and found references on Google and by using my own hands. With this piece, I really wanted to practice drawing inanimate objects because I usually focus my subject matter more on faces and landscapes. It was nice to get out of my comfort zone to draw these fruits and the AirPods and to practice painting more hands.

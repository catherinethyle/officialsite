"Lay With Me Beneath the Gemini Sky". 2020. Blender 3D, A-Frame, & Adobe Photoshop.

<--

-->

Dreams. We all have them no matter how big or small. Dreams are vast, boundless, beautiful, and mysterious- just like space. Being heavily based upon stargazing, the surreal landscape of "Lay With Me Beneath the Gemini Sky" transports the viewer to the middle of a plane surrounded by mountains, located under a cloudy blue night sky. The scene is accompanied by constellations and a red moon, all while ambient music and the sound of whirring wind plays in the background, providing a visual and auditory experience for the viewer. The title “Lay with Me Beneath the Gemini Sky” derives from the fact that the moon phase and constellations shown are based on those present on my birthday (May 23, 2002), with Gemini season falling from May 21 to June 20. This piece serves as an invitation to the viewer to pursue and immerse themselves in their dreams and goals and to bask in the beauty of their potential, imagination, and efforts.

This piece was my first attempt and introduction to 3D Modeling and A-Frame. I was lucky enough to have the help and guidance of Eddson Jose to bring my vision to life. The execution of this piece wuldn't have been possible without him.

New Art City room instalation direction, moon, mountains, clouds, & sky by Catherine Thy Lê. Ambient music, New Art City room text, A-Frame production, grass, & animation by Eddson Jose

"Nanami". 2020. Acrylic on recycled cardboard. 8.25 x 5 in.

<-- -->

This is a fanart piece of an anime character named Nanami from "Kamisama Hajimemashita". I watched the anime back in the fall of 2020 and instantly fell in love with the animation style and character design. One scene in particular caught my eye and I decided to paint it. This particular scene captured Nanami very beautifully in a butterfly kimono mid-dance. She looked very angelic and serene enveloped in light and with bubbles floating surrounding her. I used this as a chance to see how well I can replicate artwork based off a reference.

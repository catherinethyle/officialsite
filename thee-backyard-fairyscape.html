"Thee Backyard Fairyscape". 2020. Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop.

<-- -->


"Thee Backyard Fairyscape" is an illustration of my backyard during sunset. While being in quarintine, there were not many places to go to or escape to. I began finding peace in how the sky changes every second, minute, and hour of the day and how each scene it presents is captivating. This piece captures how the sky, and nature in general, can have a serene effect on us mortal and finite beings. The gradient, color scheme, and soft brush strokes add to the magical and fictional aura this piece exudes.

"Thee Backyard Fairyscape" was the first piece I experiemnted in Adobe Photoshop with. Before, I would mainly create my ilustrations using Adobe Illustrator but I was inspired to break out of my comfort zone and try something new with this particular piece. I initially started in Illustrtor to create the basic shapes and colors and then used Photoshop to complete the piece by shading the illustration in a style with visable brush strokes.

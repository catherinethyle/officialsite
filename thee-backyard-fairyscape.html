"Thee Backyard Fairyscape". 2020. Adobe Illustrator & Photoshop.

<-- -->

"Thee Backyard Fairyscape" is an illustration of my backyard during sunset. While being in quarintine, there were not many places to go or escape to. I began finding peace in how the sky changes every second, minute, and hour of the day and how each scene it presents is captivating. This piece captures how the sky, and nature in general, can have a serene effect on us mortal and finite beings. The gradients, color scheme, and soft brush strokes add to the magical and fictional aura that this piece exudes.

"Thee Backyard Fairyscape" was the first piece I experimented digital painting in Adobe Photoshop with. Before, I would mainly create my illustrations using Adobe Illustrator but I was inspired to break out of my comfort zone and try something new with this particular piece. I initially started "Thee Backyard Fairyscape" in Illustrator to create the basic shapes and colors. I then used Photoshop to complete the piece through shading in a style with visible and rough brush strokes.

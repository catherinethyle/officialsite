"Decisions". 2020. Twine & Adobe Illustrator.

Click Here to Play


As someone who has always been interested in criminology, thrillers, and murder mysteries, this was a huge inspiration and driving force behind the creation of this game. "Decissions" revolves around you, the player, who has to decide what actions to take after witnessing a woman’s motorcycle being stolen. One thing leads to another and you must make an ultimate decision- one of life and death. I wanted to give the user a fun and interactive game that would keep them on their toes and engaged throughout. Along with the thrilling plot, I decided to visually enforce the game with background images that correlate to the location of each passage (which I got from Google Images and edited in Adobe Illustrator). While the subject of the game is a bit heavy and dark, the aesthetic and visuals are bright and light, creating an intended contrast and irony.

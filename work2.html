"Untitled". 2020. p5js.


Click Here to Color


Growing up, I played a lot of dress up games and filled tons of coloring pages. With this project, I wanted to create a highly simplified version of my childhood memories. This p5js piece is an interactive coloring page which shows a geometric face. When you click on the shapes, you are able to "color them in". I made it so upon multiple clicks, the color is randomized within a range so that each time, your shapes are in a slightly different color.

"Your Effort is Enough". 2020. Acrylic on canvas. 4 x 4 in.

<--

Seeing so many people on social media baking minimalist cakes and lunch box cakes that looked so cute and aesthetically pleasing, I was really inspiried and wanted to make one of my own.

"Your Effort is Enough" is an acrylic painting of a cake with an affirmation and flowers decorated on it. During the time of its creation, I was going through a bit of a rough patch and decided to paint out my emotions. It was really therepudic for me roughly finger paint and lesurly add color to this painting with a palette knife. The rough stokes, dark and pastel colors, and the white text and flowers that were born from my frustrations into a reminder that what I'm doing right now and the progress I have made is enough give me a sense of calm and peace whenever I look at it.

"Zimzalabim". 2020/2021. Adobe Photoshop & After Effects.

<--

The vibrant visuals and concept of Red Velvet's "Zimzalabim" music video entranced me when I first saw it and I wanted to have my own take on it.

Initially, I had created an illustration of two specific scenes in the music video that I liked the most back in the fall of 2020. This piece has since hed a special place in my heart. I had recently decided to come back to it and use it to learn and experiment with After Effects. For the After Effects rendition, I decided to go back and add candy canes to my illustration so I would have more elements to work with.

The lighting, texture, and vibe of the motion graphic strays from the original illustration to create a more ominous piece, which I belive pairs well with my music selection and with the rain symulation that I itched it try out.

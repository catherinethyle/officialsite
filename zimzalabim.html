"Zimzalabim". 2020/2021.

<-- -->

The vibrant visuals and concept of Red Velvet's "Zimzalabim" music video entranced me when I first saw it, resuting in me creating own spin on it.

Initially, I had created an illustration of two specific scenes in the music video that I liked the most (shown below) back in the fall of 2020. I combined the two shots to create one illustration with a grainy overlay (inspired by 90's anime style edits). This piece has since held a special place in my heart. I recently decided to come back to this piece and use it to learn and experiment with After Effects. For the motion graphic rendition, I decided to go back and add candy canes to my illustration so I would have more elements to work with.

The lighting, texture, and vibe of the motion graphic strays from the original illustration to create a darker and more ominous piece.The new look pairs well with my music selection (a cover by "Stimpy" on Youtube) and with the rain simulation that I itched to try out.
